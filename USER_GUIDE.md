## Creating an Account

### Step-by-Step Registration

1. **Open the app** and tap on **"Don't have an account? Sign Up"**

2. **Fill in your details**:
   - **Full Name**: Enter your full name
   - **Email Address**: Enter a valid email address
   - **Password**: Create a strong password (at least 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password

3. **Tap "Sign Up"**

4. **Verification**: Check your email for a verification link (if email verification is enabled)

5. **Success**: Once registered, you'll be automatically logged in and taken to the dashboard

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Make sure to use a valid email address as you may need it for password recovery.

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 6 characters
- Use a combination of letters and numbers for better security
- Keep your password safe and don't share it with anyone

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### App Won't Install

**Problem**: APK installation fails

**Solutions**:
- Ensure "Unknown Sources" or "Install Unknown Apps" is enabled
- Check that you have enough storage space (at least 100MB free)
- Try downloading the APK again if the file might be corrupted
- Make sure your Android version is 5.0 or higher

---

#### Can't Log In

**Problem**: Login fails with correct credentials

**Solutions**:
- Check that you're entering the correct email and password
- Ensure you have an active internet connection
- Try resetting your password using "Forgot Password?"
- Check if your email has been verified (check your inbox/spam)

---

#### App Crashes or Freezes

**Problem**: App stops working or becomes unresponsive

**Solutions**:
- Close and restart the app
- Clear the app cache: Settings â†’ Apps â†’ Online Bakery â†’ Clear Cache
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Update to the latest version of the app if available
- Restart your device

---

#### Products Not Loading

**Problem**: Product list is empty or won't load

**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection
- Pull down to refresh the products list
- Sign out and sign back in
- Clear app cache and try again

---

#### Images Not Displaying

**Problem**: Product images appear broken or don't load

**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection
- Wait a few moments for images to load
- Ensure you're not in data saver mode
- Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data

---

#### Profile Update Fails

**Problem**: Can't save profile changes

**Solutions**:
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check your internet connection
- Try again after a few moments
- Sign out and sign back in
